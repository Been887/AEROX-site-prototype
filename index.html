Selecting a CAD Modeler

A lot of thought was put into the design of the car.
At first I tried to use Blender to create a model, which was a challenge to navigate as a beginner, only to realise that Blender is built around renders not specifications and parameters, and the car I made with it had a long way to go.

My second thought was to learn how to CAD, but which one? FreeCad. Not an amazing first choice but allowed me to learn the foundations of Computer Aided Design, locally (but with weird function names).

After a few progress losses, crashes and failed intersections i moved to Onshape (student license), which i would reccomend, although it seemed very confusing with assemblies and part studios, it does not crash or lag nearly as much as freecad (on a school laptop) and was easy to me after learning so much software.
