A Refined approach to STEM Racing
A team of 4 competing in STEM Racing Pro Class.
the team - Avi, Ben, Shaan and Seb.

Most teams would dive straight in and get designing 'aero' cars -
aerox takes a more careful and tested approach, working as a team to create the best branding and design.
Our expertise spans over various topics, for example >
Avijeet is our expert writer and graphic designer, whose experience and eye for detail provided us sponsors and professionalism.
Seb is our team manager and manufacturing head, organising deadlines accurately and ensuring that work is finished to a high standard. His manufacturing abilities produced well finished components.
Shaan is Avijeet's partner in securing sponsors; having secured 2/3 of our sponsored funds. He also provides an unbiased opinion and is more than happy to help with any struggles, no matter how niche.
Ben is the designer and technical manager; he is responsible for the design of the car and it's paintjob, rendering, the team video, coding the website and general creative or technical tasks.

This allows us to have an exceedingly efficient workflow, for each person to focus on their job while interacting with each other as a team, to become the best at STEM Racing.

As a team, we overcome struggles and strive to be the best we can be. Challenges pose an obstacle and learning opportunity rather than a hindrance.

About Us - A brief history

Aerox racing have been working as a team since Year 7. The first first car at entry class looked great but had miles to go. It was created using blender, which is a render tool focussed on visuals and sculpting - definitely not cars fit to a specification. The car itself was overweight and extremely slow, but claimed a spot on the podium due to following regulations (loosely). Points were also claimed via other methods e.g a sleek enterprise portfolio.
As a team, we worked half as well as this season. This is why the majority of our 7 members left after entry class, not finding the thrill of the competition or efficiency of teamwork like we do now. some of us regrouped during the start of pro class - Seb kept the team going.
